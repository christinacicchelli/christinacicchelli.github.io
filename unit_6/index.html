Christina Simpson-Cicchelli is a Connecticut-born writer, web designer, and artist. Her professional background is as diverse as her writing portfolio, though she excels in creating content that strikes a cord with any given audience.


With a bold and honest voice, Christina has never shied away from being vulnerable. This is reflected both in her life and in her poems about love, family, and trauma. She graduated from Fairfield University with an MFA in Creative Writing. Christina now lives in Brooklyn, NY where she doodles and binge watches tv shows on Hulu.
